Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

What We Collect

When you use Signal by RIG, we collect:

  • Assessment answers: Your responses to career readiness questions, including career clarity, resume status, LinkedIn presence, and interview readiness.
  • Profile information: Name, email, job title, experience level, salary expectations, and location preferences you choose to provide.
  • Oracle verification: If you verify as an Oracle Health employee, we store your email domain for verification purposes only.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, and interaction patterns to improve the product.
  • Device information: Browser type, screen size, and general location (city-level) for analytics.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate your career readiness assessment and personalized recommendations
  • To improve our scoring algorithms and product features
  • To send you assessment results and relevant career resources (with your consent)
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns (never individual data) for product improvement

What We Don't Do

  • We never sell your personal data to third parties
  • We never share individual assessment results without your explicit consent
  • We never use your data for advertising
  • We never access your LinkedIn, email, or other accounts without your permission

Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL) hosted in the United States. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Assessment data is retained until you request deletion.

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all data we hold about you at any time
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information
  • Deletion: You can request complete deletion of your data by emailing privacy@rodgersintelligence.com
  • Export: You can export your assessment data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of analytics tracking at any time

Oracle Health Employee Data

If you verify as an Oracle Health employee, we store only your email domain for verification. We do not store your full Oracle email address unless you explicitly provide it. Free tier access is granted based on domain verification and can be revoked if abused.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality and optional analytics cookies (PostHog) to understand usage patterns. You can opt out of analytics cookies at any time.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (Privacy: supabase.com/privacy)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (Privacy: stripe.com/privacy)
  • PostHog: Product analytics (Privacy: posthog.com/privacy)
  • Resend: Email delivery (Privacy: resend.com/privacy)
  • Vercel: Hosting and deployment (Privacy: vercel.com/privacy)

Data Retention

Assessment data is retained for 2 years or until you request deletion. Free tier data follows the same policy. Paid subscription data is retained for the duration of the subscription plus 90 days for billing purposes.

Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: privacy@rodgersintelligence.com
