
## 6. SCORING FRAMEWORK

### Total Score Architecture:
- **Overall Score:** Aggregate rating (e.g., 85/100)
- **Section Scores:** Breakdown by resume section:
  - Summary/Objective
  - Experience
  - Education
  - Skills
- **Line Scores:** Individual bullet point ratings
- **Keyword Match:** ATS keyword alignment percentage

### Score Categories:
- **Impact Language:** verb strength and quantification
- **Keyword Match:** ATS optimization alignment
- **ATS Optimization:** format and structure
- **Readability:** clarity and scannability
- **Leadership Narrative:** career progression story

### Score Interpretation:
- **90-100:** Excellent, top 10% of applicants
- **80-89:** Good, competitive application
- **70-79:** Average, needs improvement
- **Below 70:** Significant issues, likely filtered

## 10. DESIGN PATTERNS TO REPLICATE

1. **30-Second Turnaround:**
   - Instant feedback vs. slow human review
   - Key differentiator for busy job seekers
   - Sets market expectation for speed

2. **Line-by-Line Analysis:**
   - Granular feedback > general feedback
   - Shows deep analysis capability
   - More actionable for users

3. **Active vs Passive Voice Detection:**
   - Specific writing improvement criteria
   - Educational + actionable
   - Measurable improvement

4. **Score Breakdown by Section:**
   - Resume divided into clear sections
   - Section scores aggregate to total
   - Identifies specific improvement areas

5. **Funnel Framework:**
   - Top (LinkedIn) → Middle (Resume) → Bottom (Offers)
   - Positions tool at every career stage
   - Aligns features to user journey

6. **Targeted Optimization:**
   - Job description paste functionality
   - Keyword gap identification
   - Specific tailoring for roles

7. **LinkedIn as Top of Funnel:**
   - Recruiter visibility leads to opportunities
   - Personal brand enhancement
   - Inbound opportunity generation

8. **B2B Institutional Channel:**
   - Universities and bootcamps as B2B customers
   - Scalable career services delivery
   - Recurring revenue model

9. **Section-Level Scoring:**
   - Summary, Experience, Education, Skills scored separately
   - Drill-down capability
   - Targeted improvement

10. **Before/After Feedback:**
    - Specific suggestions with explanations
    - Shows what's wrong and how to fix
    - Line-level recommendations
