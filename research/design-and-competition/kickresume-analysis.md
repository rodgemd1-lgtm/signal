**Core Business:** AI resume/cover letter builder + personal website builder  
**Target User:** Design-conscious job seekers, professionals who value visual first impressions

## 8. GAPS FOR SIGNAL TO EXPLOIT

1. **No Salary Intelligence:**
   - Beautiful resumes but no compensation data
   - No market value assessment
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Salary benchmarking, career value calculation

2. **No Job Search Tracking:**
   - Helps create great documents but not track applications
   - No application pipeline
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Interview tracking, status updates

3. **No Interview Preparation:**
   - Gets you to interview but doesn't prepare you
   - No company research
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Interview coaching, hiring manager insights

4. **No Networking Tools:**
   - Great for documents, not for relationship building
   - No warm outreach
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Connection facilitation, warm outreach templates

5. **No Offer Support:**
   - Landing is the goal, negotiating not addressed
   - No benefits analysis
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Offer letter analysis, negotiation coaching

6. **No Market Intelligence:**
   - Resume in vacuum, not market context
   - Static guidance
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** In-demand skills tracking, industry trends

7. **No Recruiter Connection:**
   - Pulls lever but not who pulls it
   - No inbound opportunity tracking
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Recruiter insights, hiring manager profiles

8. **Personal Website is Brochure:**
   - One-click conversion is great
   - Resulting site is static
   - No analytics or engagement
   - **SIGNAL OPPORTUNITY:** Dynamic career portfolio with analytics
